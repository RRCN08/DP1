Ishān Dhvani

a brief inquiry into the Assamese language

As my first Semester Thesis Project, I have created Ishān Dhvani, which aims to represent the various dialects, demographics, and recordings of the Assamese language in a medium. This has been achieved by collecting speech data from native speakers of Assamese from various geographical locations in Assam.



Welcome

We have compiled audio recordings from 60 people across Assam, from west to east, in a search of the different dialects of the Assamese language.
